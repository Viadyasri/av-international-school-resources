Essential Questions to Ask Before Choosing a CBSE School
Overview

Selecting the Best CBSE School in Secunderabad involves much more than comparing brochures or online reviews. One of the best ways to evaluate a school is by asking the right questions during a campus visit or admission counselling session.

This resource provides a practical list of questions that parents can use while comparing the Best CBSE Schools Near Secunderabad, Telangana and making an informed admission decision.

Questions About Academics
How does the school implement the CBSE curriculum?
Does the teaching methodology focus on conceptual learning?
How is student progress assessed throughout the academic year?
What support is available for students who require additional academic guidance?
How are practical learning and project-based activities incorporated into the curriculum?
Questions About Teachers
What is the average teaching experience of the faculty?
How often do teachers receive professional development or training?
What is the student-teacher ratio?
How are students encouraged to ask questions and participate in class?
Questions About Student Development

Parents should also understand how the school supports learning beyond academics.

Ask about:

Sports and physical education
Music, dance and arts
Leadership opportunities
Public speaking activities
Clubs and competitions
Community service initiatives
Questions About Infrastructure

During the campus visit, ask about:

Smart classrooms
Science laboratories
Computer laboratories
Library facilities
Sports infrastructure
Medical support
Transportation
Questions About Safety

Student safety is an essential factor.

Consider asking:

What security measures are in place?
Is CCTV available across the campus?
How are visitors monitored?
What emergency response procedures are followed?
Questions About Parent Communication

Good schools maintain transparent communication with families.

Ask:

How often are parent-teacher meetings conducted?
How are academic updates shared?
Is there a parent communication portal or mobile application?
Final Checklist

Before making a decision, ensure that you have:

Visited the campus
Spoken with teachers
Observed classroom interaction
Compared multiple schools
Understood the teaching philosophy
Considered your child's interests and learning style
